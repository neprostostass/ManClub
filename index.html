    Our experienced hairdressers, stylists of men's haircuts at the Man Club barbershop, are always in trend: men's haircut, platform, pompadour, hedgehog, beaver, canadian, bob and many others!

    Classic men's model haircut

    Men's haircuts in this style always look neat and elegant. This haircut will suit many, besides the classic is always in fashion

    The main advantages of this style are unpretentious styling, and therefore minimal time spent on maintenance. These can be haircuts in which the length of the hair is the same all over the head. Haircuts with the shortest possible hair on the sides and above the ears or a haircut in the "Preppy" style are quite popular.

    Men's haircut semi-box

    In the half-box haircut, there are two edgings: one at the extreme hairline, and the second along its upper edge (it goes into the temporal zone and the back of the head)

    A semi-box is most suitable for owners of dark hair. The semi-boxing implies an average length at the crown and almost “naked” temples, while the transition is smooth and soft. An important feature of the semi-box is the location of the edging on the border of the back of the head or slightly below it.

    Men's haircut box

    Men's haircut box has a shorter hair length. It is concise, short and dynamic, like a boxer's punch. Men's haircut box can very advantageously emphasize the strength and courage of its owner, his tough character, willpower and strong body

    This is one of the few haircuts that suits absolutely any type of face. Looks good on blond hair, especially with an ashy tint. One of the few haircuts that is suitable for thin hair

    Men's haircut platform

    The site, perhaps, ideally combines the features of masculinity and concern for appearance. She neatly looks and resembles any image, while not overloading with intentional savagery or vice versa femininity

    Best of all, the playground looks built from thick and elastic hair. The initial hair length for this brutal hairstyle should be about 4-5 centimeters

    Men's haircut with iroquis

    The hair should stand evenly vertically or at a slight angle from the hairline and up to the occiput.

    The platform is a perfectly flat surface, as if beveling the natural roundness of the human skull. But the hairs from the sides and behind are cut with scissors or shaved with a machine to an ultra-short state.
